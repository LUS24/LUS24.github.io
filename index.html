About me

Hi, my name is Leo and I'm a Pharmacist and Biochemist that switched careers to Data Science and Python development. I worked as a laboratory assistant in a research group specialized in environmental chemistry. During that time I collaborated by taking marine sediments samples from Antarctica and analyzing the resulting data using the R programming language. I collaborated with other research groups to design experiments related to plan biotechnology and analyze the data from those experiments. After learning Python, I started working on other projects where I used Selenium WebDriver to automate data gathering from websites and storing the data in MongoDB database running locally with Docker. I created a web app using Django where users can upload their datasets and share them with other peers.
